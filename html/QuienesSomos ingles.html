About Us

"Discover the Beauty of Antioquia"

Mission



At Rutas Paisas, our mission is to be your guide to explore and experience the cultural, natural, and historical richness of Antioquia.


We are committed to providing authentic and memorable tourist experiences, connecting travelers with the wonders of this land.


Our goal is to promote sustainable and responsible tourism, respecting and preserving the heritage of Antioquia while providing exceptional service to our visitors.





Vision



Our vision at Rutas Paisas is to become the leading reference in tourism in Antioquia, known for our passion for the region, excellence in our services, and the promotion of the natural and cultural beauty of this destination.


We want to inspire travelers from around the world to discover and fall in love with Antioquia, contributing to the sustainable development of the region and the happiness of those who visit it.


By 2025, we have plans to expand our portfolio of destinations, becoming not only a regional reference but also a national one.





Objectives



To promote awareness of the various places in the department of Antioquia, encouraging responsible tourism within our territory, involving the local population, showcasing their cultures, and taking advantage of the high flow of foreign visitors who visit our country every day.
