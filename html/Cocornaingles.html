Cocorná Package - Adventures in Nature


Cocorná is a municipality founded in 1830, located in the eastern part of Antioquia. It is a destination for nature and adventure lovers.


Tourist Sites: The Cocorná viewpoint, the 'Charco del Amor' (Pool of Love), and we also find the Melcocho River, which is considered the clearest river in South America.



Cocorná Package - Adventures in Nature


Duration: 2 days, 1 night.


Includes:

  • Excursion to the Melcocho River, hiking activities, and a visit to the Yellow Bridge.
  • Nature observation activities.
  • Lodging in riverside cabins.
  • Bonfire night and traditional music.
