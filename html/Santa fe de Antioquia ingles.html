Santa Fe de Antioquia - History and Relaxation


Santa Fe de Antioquia is a historical gem, founded in 1541. Located an hour and a half from Medellin, Santa Fe de Antioquia can be reached by road, heading towards the Antioquia Urabá region. It has a warm climate that ranges from 25 to 35 degrees Celsius, where you will be amazed by the lush vegetation in the city's parks and the ornate houses that you see when walking through its cobbled streets under the blue sky.


Tourist attractions: The Puente de Occidente (Western Bridge), the Metropolitan Basilica Cathedral, the Main Park, and the Juan del Corral Museum.
