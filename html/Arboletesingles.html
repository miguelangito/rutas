Discover Arboletes: A Tropical Paradise in the Northern Extremity of Urabá, Antioquia


Arboletes is a municipality in Colombia, located in the Urabá region in the department of Antioquia. It shares (yet to be defined) boundaries with the Department of Córdoba. Its municipal seat is located on the shores of the Caribbean Sea and is 472 kilometers away from the city of Medellín, the capital of the department of Antioquia.


The main attraction of Arboletes:The main attraction of Arboletes is the mud volcano located 1 kilometer from the urban center of the municipality and on the seashore. This site is recognized as the only mud volcano located on the seashore in Colombia.
