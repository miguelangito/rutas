"San Luis - Extreme Adventure"


San Luis is known as the 'Green Pearl of the East,' founded in 1875. It is located in the midst of a highly biodiverse ecosystem with many wildlife species, some of them endemic.


Tourist Sites: La Cuba Waterfall, San Luis Gonzaga Church, Dormilón River, and La Planta Pool 'The Largest Pool in Eastern Antioquia'.
