Travel Back in Time in the Picturesque Town of Jardín


Jardín is a picturesque town founded in 1863. Its colonial architecture and cobblestone streets transport you to bygone eras. It's famous for its coffee, beautiful mountain landscapes, and waterfalls.


Tourist Sites: The Cave of Splendor, the Minor Basilica of the Immaculate Conception, and the Main Park. Discover the magic of Jardín and let yourself be captivated by its historical and natural charm!



Jardín Package - 'Antioquia's Natural Charm'


Duration: 3 days, 2 nights.

  • Accommodation in a typical regional inn.
