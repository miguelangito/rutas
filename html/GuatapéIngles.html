Guatapé's Treasures: Between the Rock and the Color


Guatapé is known for its stunning reservoir, the Guatapé Reservoir, which was created in 1972. The town is famous for its colorful architecture and the Piedra del Peñol, a massive rock formation.


Among its main tourist attractions are the Piedra del Peñol, the Guatapé Boardwalk, and the Guatapé Reservoir.


Guatapé Package - 'Adventure at Piedra del Peñol'
