Explore Jericó, the Birthplace of Colombia's First Saint


Jericó, founded in 1850, is known as the birthplace of Mother Laura, Colombia's first saint. It is a charming town with a rich history.


Tourist Sites: The Birthplace House of Mother Laura, the Basilica of the Immaculate Conception, the Main Park, and the Museum of the House of Culture. Come and discover the historical and spiritual essence of Jericó



Jericó Package - 'Pilgrimage and Spirituality'


Duration: 3 days, 2 nights.


Includes:

  • Visit to the Birthplace House of Mother Laura, tour of the Basilica of the Immaculate Conception, and hikes in the surroundings of Jericó.
