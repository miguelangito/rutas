Adventures in Río Claro: Discover the Colombian Rainforest


Río Claro in Doradal, Antioquia, Colombia, has a rich history dating back to pre-Columbian times and stood out in the 19th century as a key transportation hub due to the railroad.


Today, it is a renowned tourist destination famous for its crystal-clear river and stunning caves.


Some of its main tourist attractions include marble caves, bird and wildlife watching, tubing and rafting on the river, and enjoying the natural beauty.
