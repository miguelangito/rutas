Discover the Historical Charm of Granada


Granada, founded in 1862, was originally known for its coffee production, and its economy was based on agriculture. Over the years, Granada has preserved its traditional charm.


Tourist Sites: Plaza de Bolívar, La Chapa viewpoint, Santa Bárbara parish. Immerse yourself in the history and beauty of Granada in every corner of this charming town!



Granada Package - 'Discovering Granada'


Duration: 3 days, 2 nights.


Includes:


  • Visit to Plaza de Bolívar and tour of the Santa Bárbara Church.
  • Lodging in a cozy inn in the center of Granada.
  • Excursion to the La Chapa viewpoint to enjoy panoramic views.
  • Tasting of local coffee at a nearby farm.
  • Traditional Antioquian meals included.
