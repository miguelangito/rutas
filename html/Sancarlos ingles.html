San Carlos: A Colonial Gem in the Mountains of Antioquia


San Carlos is a picturesque town located in the mountains of Antioquia. Founded in 1793, it has preserved its colonial charm and is renowned for its natural surroundings.


Among its main tourist attractions are the Main Park, the San Carlos Borromeo Church, and the hiking trails, ideal for bird watching, that extend throughout the surroundings.
