"San Rafael - Retreat in Nature"


San Rafael, located in the reservoir area, is a municipality rich in water sources. Abundant streams and pools are its main features, and its River Festivals, celebrated every January, are famous.


Tourist Sites: Arenal River, Bizcocho River, La Araña Pool, Churimo River, La Cazuela Pools, Isleta, and Golondrinas.



San Rafael Package - 'Retreat in Nature


Duration: 2 days and 1 night.


Includes:

  • Hiking at La Cazuela, Isleta, and Golondrinas Pools.
  • Accommodation at an ecological farm.
  • Outdoor relaxation and yoga sessions.
  • Tasting of traditional Antioquia dishes.
